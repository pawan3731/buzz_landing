Privacy Policy

1. Data and Information Collected & Use
When expressing an interest in obtaining additional information about the Services or registering to use the Services, BUUZ App requires you to provide the Company with personal contact information, such as name, phone number and email address (“Required Contact Information”). Required Contact Information, Billing Information, and Optional Information about Customers are referred to collectively as “Data About BUUZ Software Inc. Customers”, or in the case of Attendees, “Data About BUUZ Software Inc. Attendees”. As you navigate the Company’s websites, BUUZ Software Inc. may also collect information through the use of commonly-used information-gathering tools, such as cookies and web beacons (“Web Site Navigational Information”). Website Navigational Information includes standard information from your web browser (such as browser type and browser language), your Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, and the actions you take on the Company’s Web sites (such as the Web pages viewed and the links clicked).

Purpose of Collection
We collect personal data exclusively for the purpose of facilitating event and place suggestions within the app and website. The collected information is utilized solely to customize the shared or available information for enhanced user experience.

Restricted Use of Personal Data
Your name, email, phone number, and other personal data are strictly utilized for the aforementioned purpose and are not employed for any other activities. We uphold the highest standards of confidentiality and refrain from utilizing your data for advertising purposes within our platform.

Limited Data Collection
We adhere to a policy of minimal data collection and only acquire information explicitly provided by users during account verification or when furnishing specific event details. This approach ensures that data is gathered with the user's explicit consent and for specific, well-defined purposes.

User Consent and Opt-In
Users are required to explicitly opt-in to the data collection process, ensuring informed and voluntary consent. This opt-in process is specifically designed for account verification or to enhance the provision of more specific event information.

Data Protection
We prioritize the security and protection of user data through robust security measures. Our commitment to safeguarding user information extends to refraining from any form of advertising within our platform, ensuring a focused and secure user experience.

2. Web Site Navigational Information Cookies, Web Beacons and IP Addresses
BUUZ Software Inc. uses commonly-used information-gathering tools, such as cookies and Web beacons, to collect information as you navigate the Company’s Web sites (“Web Site Navigational Information”). This section describes the types of Web Site Navigational Information used on the Company’s Web sites and how this information may be used.

Cookies
BUUZ Software Inc. uses cookies to make interactions with the Company’s Web sites easy and meaningful. When you visit one of the Company’s Web sites, BUUZ Software Inc’s servers send a cookie to your computer. Standing alone, cookies do not personally identify you; they merely recognize your Web browser. Unless you choose to identify yourself to BUUZ Software Inc., either by responding to a promotional offer, opening an account, or filling out a Web form (such as a “Contact Me”), you remain anonymous to the Company. BUUZ Software Inc. uses cookies that are session-based and persistent-based. Session cookies exist only during one session. They disappear from your computer when you close your browser software or turn off your computer. Persistent cookies remain on your computer after you close your browser or turn off your computer. Please note that if you disable your Web browser’s ability to accept cookies, you will be able to navigate the Company’s Web sites, but you will not be able to successfully use the Services. Targeting or Advertising Cookies
From time-to-time, BUUZ Software Inc. engages third parties to track and analyze usage and volume statistical information from individuals who visit the Company’s Web sites. BUUZ Software Inc. sometimes uses cookies delivered by third parties to track the performance of Company advertisements. For example, these cookies remember which browsers have visited the Company’s Web sites. The information provided to third parties does not include personal information, but this information may be re-associated with personal information after the Company receives it. BUUZ Software Inc. also contracts with third-party advertising networks that collect IP addresses and other information from Web beacons (see below) on the Company’s Web sites, from emails, and on third-party Web sites. Ad networks follow your online activities over time by collecting Web Site Navigational Information through automated means, including through the use of cookies. They use this information to provide advertisements about products and services tailored to your interests. You may see these advertisements on other Web sites. This process also helps us manage and track the effectiveness of our marketing efforts.

Web Beacons
BUUZ Software Inc. uses Web beacons alone or in conjunction with cookies to compile information about Customers and Visitors’ usage of the Company’s Web sites and interaction with emails from the Company. Web beacons are clear electronic images that can recognize certain types of information on your computer, such as cookies, when you viewed a particular Web site tied to the Web beacon, and a description of a Web site tied to the Web beacon. For example, BUUZ Software Inc. may place Web beacons in marketing emails that notify the Company when you click on a link in the email that directs you to one of the Company’s Web sites. BUUZ Software Inc. uses Web beacons to operate and improve the Company’s Web sites and email communications.

IP Addresses
When you visit BUUZ Software’s Web sites, the Company collects your Internet Protocol (“IP”) addresses to track and aggregate non-personal information. For example, BUUZ Software Inc. uses IP addresses to monitor the regions from which Customers and Visitors navigate the Company’s websites. BUUZ Software Inc. also collects IP addresses from Customers whey they log into the Services as part of the Company’s “Identity Confirmation” and “IP Range Restrictions” security features.

Do Not Track
Currently, various browsers – including Internet Explorer, Firefox, and Safari – offer a “do not track” or “DNT” option that relies on a technology known as a DNT header, which sends a signal to Web sites’ visited by the user about the user’s browser DNT preference setting. BUUZ Software Inc. does not currently commit to responding to browsers’ DNT signals with respect to the Company’s Web sites, in part, because no common industry standard for DNT has been adopted by industry groups, technology companies or regulators, including no consistent standard of interpreting user intent. BUUZ Software Inc. takes privacy and meaningful choice seriously and will make efforts to continue to monitor developments around DNT browser technology and the implementation of a standard.
