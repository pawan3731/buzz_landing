How It Works

Find Events, Venues & More
BUUZ helps you discover live music, sports, nightlife, top tourist attractions, and soon—happy hour specials! Whenever you’re looking for something fun, BUUZ has you covered.

Personalized Just for You
The more you use BUUZ, the smarter it gets! The app learns what you love—whether it’s indie rock, football, dog-friendly events, or food festivals. But don’t worry, we never share or sell your data—our pledge to you.

Earn Coins & Unlock Perks
You’ll earn coins for swiping, suggesting venues, inviting friends or playing Trivia. More coins mean access to exclusive invitations—think tequila tastings, private concerts, and pop-up dining experiences.

Get Smart Event Suggestions
Hate blues music? You won’t get a blues event suggested. Love Halloween parties, flea markets, or soccer? BUUZ will keep you in the loop with the best events near you.

Fast, easy, and completely free. Start exploring today!
